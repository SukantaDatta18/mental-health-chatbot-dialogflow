Mental Health & Wellness Tips

Small changes can make a big difference in your mental wellbeing

Daily Habits

Morning Routine

Start your day with 5 minutes of stretching and deep breathing to set a positive tone.

Balanced Nutrition

Include omega-3s (fish, nuts), whole grains, and leafy greens in your diet for brain health.

Quality Sleep

Aim for 7-9 hours, maintain a consistent schedule, and create a relaxing bedtime routine.

Mindfulness & Exercise

Mindfulness Meditation

Practice 10 minutes daily to reduce stress and increase emotional regulation.

Yoga for Stress Relief

Try simple poses like Child's Pose or Legs-Up-the-Wall to calm your nervous system.

Nature Walks

30-minute walks in green spaces can significantly boost mood and reduce anxiety.

Mood-Boosting Foods

Leafy Greens

Spinach and kale are rich in folate which helps produce dopamine, a feel-good neurotransmitter.

Berries

High in antioxidants that reduce inflammation linked to depression and anxiety.

Dark Chocolate

Contains flavonoids that increase blood flow to the brain and boost mood (in moderation).

Crisis Resources

If you or someone you know is struggling, these resources can help:

National Suicide Prevention Lifeline

Call 999 (BAN) for 24/7 free and confidential support

Crisis Text Line

Text HOME to 741741 to connect with a crisis counselor

Find a Therapist

Visit Psychology Today to search for professionals in your area
